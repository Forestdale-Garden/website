3/20/2024

Our first meeting!

Our first meeting on 3/20/2024 at Forestdale Elementary School was a success! The goal of our first meeting was to meet with homeowners abutting the vacant lot and introduce the idea of the garden, and get their feedback. This important first step helps us understand what the requirements of the garden will be, and we were lucky to have awesome participation. Some of our neighbors even helped spread the word when we had a hiccup with our mailings - thank you!

We learned that maintaining the garden was the number one concern, especially with regards to pest management, the local coyote population, and ensuring the burden of upkeep is shared by more than one person. We also learned that it is important to keep costs low so as not to impact the neighborhood financially. We're already planning to ensure that these things are addressed - check out our FAQ page for complete, up to date details on how we're ensuring we address these points.

Our next meeting will open the discussion up to more community members, and our goal will be to get more volunteers and support to make this idea sustainable!
