About the Garden

Welcome to Forestdale Neighborhood Garden! Rooted in the spirit of community and sustainability, our garden aims to bring us closer together by discovering shared roots. Our goal is to create a vibrant space on vacant land where neighbors come together to cultivate not just plants, but also friendships and a sense of belonging. We aim to create a place where individuals of all ages can reconnect with nature, learn about gardening, and share in the joys of harvesting fresh produce. Join us as we sow the seeds of a greener, more connected community in our neighborhood.

Goals

  • Reduce waste
  • Promote healthier eating
  • Share culture
