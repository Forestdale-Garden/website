Project Leadership

Ashley Kolodziej

Project and Garden Lead

Ashley Kolodziej is a part-time professor at Boston University and full-time design systems manager at Moody's Analytics. She has been gardening since she was a child, when her dad taught her how to grow potatoes, zucchini, pumpkins, and tomatoes. As a hobbyist, her gardening experience includes hydroponics, raised beds, chemical-free pest and weed management, seed saving, low-water techniques, propagation, and grafting with a focus on edible plants. A former creative director and manager, she also brings project planning and leadership experience to the table, passion and energy to motivate people to action, and an eye towards design, color, and beauty.

I'm excited to show people how beautiful and productive even small spaces like this can be, and pass on the gardening knowledge my dad gave me. My dream is to give neighbors in the area a space to work together to express their voices and interests through plants. I love cooking, so I would love to see edible plants like herbs. Some neighbors I've talked to are passionate about supporting local pollinators and growing flowers. Plants can even express our personal history and culture, from the herbs we use in our everyday cooking to the different gardening and growth practices we use across cultures, such as bonsai and ollas. It's no surprise to me how closely intertwined gardening practices are with art forms. This small space can represent us and help us learn more about each other, and the best part is that it can grow and change over time, just like our neighborhood does.

Ari Taylor

Ward 5 City Councillor

Ari Taylor is the Malden Ward 5 City Councillor and an active supporter and advocate for community-led projects. As a lifelong resident of Ward 5, Ari has been a dedicated community volunteer in Malden for more than 30 years. She ensures residents' voices are heard and that Ward 5 gets the resources it needs, and works to strengthen our sense of community by hosting neighborhood social events, ward meetings and office hours, and maintaining the Malden Ward 5 website so residents can stay informed.
