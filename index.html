Welcome to the Forestdale Neighborhood Garden Project!

The goal of this project is to use a small parcel of city-owned land as an opportunity to create community around the shared act of gardening and neighborhood care. By working together, we can take a small space and make it meaningful for the residents of Forest Street, Goodwin Avenue, and surrounding areas.

Goals

  • Reduce waste
  • Promote healthier eating
  • Share culture

The space

Benefits of a garden

This communal garden offers countless benefits, from promoting healthier lifestyles and fostering environmental stewardship to creating opportunities for social interaction and cultural exchange.
